About

Dr. Lucas Lu is a serial entrepreneur, investor, and technologist with over two decades of experience across physics research, internet platforms, social media, and AI.

He founded 5Miles in 2014 and Cybermiles (CMT) in 2017. At Alibaba Group, he led the rebuilding of Taobao's search engine, advertising platform, and wireless business unit.

Today he serves as CEO of ByteTrade Lab, an AI and robotics-focused technology company, and Chairman of Clapper, one of the top 5 social media platforms in the United States, where AI is central to the content and creator experience.

He holds a Ph.D. in Physics from Southern Methodist University and a B.S. from the University of Science and Technology of China.
