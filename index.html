About Me

Dr. Lucas Lu is a seasoned serial entrepreneur, investor, and technology visionary with over two decades of experience spanning physics research, internet platforms, social media, and Web3 innovation.

He founded 5Miles, a C2C marketplace platform, in 2014, and Cybermiles (CMT) in 2017. As a key executive at Alibaba Group, he led the transformation of Taobao's core infrastructure — including its search engine, advertising platform, and wireless business unit.

Today, Dr. Lu serves as CEO of ByteTrade Lab and Chairman of Clapper, one of the top 5 social media platforms in the United States, building at the intersection of AI, robotics, and the creator economy.

With a Ph.D. in Physics from Southern Methodist University and a B.S. from the University of Science and Technology of China, he bridges deep scientific thinking with entrepreneurial execution.
